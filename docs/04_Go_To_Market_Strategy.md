# BizPilot — Go-To-Market Strategy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** March 6, 2026  
**Target Market:** United States Small Businesses

## 1. Launch Strategy Overview

### Launch in 3 Phases

```
Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Month 1-2 of development)
  → Build hype, collect waitlist, validate demand

Phase 2: Beta Launch (Month 4-5)
  → 100-500 beta users, iterate on feedback

Phase 3: Public Launch (Month 5-6)
  → Full launch, growth engine activated
```

## 2. Pre-Launch Strategy (Start NOW while building)

### 2.1 Landing Page + Waitlist
**Launch within 1 week of starting development**

**Landing Page Must-Haves:**
- Clear value proposition: "Stop juggling 8 apps. Run your business on autopilot."
- 60-second product demo video (screen mockups + narration)
- Feature highlights with icons
- Pricing preview (builds trust)
- Social proof (even if it's "Join 500+ businesses on the waitlist")
- Waitlist signup form (email + business type)
- Referral system: "Move up the waitlist — share with friends"

**Tools:**
- Build with Next.js (part of our monorepo) OR use a quick builder like Framer
- Waitlist: Built-in (our WaitlistEntry model) or use Waitlist.me
- Analytics: PostHog

**Goal:** 1,000+ waitlist signups before beta launch

## 3. Beta Launch Strategy

### 3.1 Beta Cohort Selection
- Pick 100-500 users from waitlist
- Prioritize: solo freelancers, consultants, coaches (our primary persona)
- Geographic focus: US only initially
- Offer: **3 months free Professional plan** in exchange for feedback

### 3.2 Beta Feedback Loop
```
Weekly Feedback:
  → In-app feedback button (always visible)
  → Weekly 5-question survey (email)
  → Bi-weekly 15-min user interviews (10 users)
  → Track all feature requests in public roadmap
  
Metrics to Track:
  → Activation rate (completed onboarding)
  → Feature adoption (which features used most)
  → Retention (daily/weekly active)
  → NPS score (target: >50)
  → Time saved per week (user-reported)
```

### 3.3 Beta-to-Paid Conversion
- Week 8: Remind users trial ending in 4 weeks
- Week 10: Show value recap: "You've sent 23 invoices, collected $12,400, saved 45 hours"
- Week 12: Convert to paid with "Beta Pioneer" discount (20% off for life)

## 6. Pricing & Conversion Strategy

### Free Trial Funnel
```
Visit landing page (100%)
  → Sign up for free trial (15-25%)
  → Complete onboarding (60-70% of signups)
  → Send first invoice or create booking page (40-50%)
  → Use product for 7+ days (30-40%)
  → Convert to paid (15-25% of trials)
```

### Conversion Tactics
1. **Value Summary Email (Day 7):** "This week, BizPilot helped you send 3 invoices worth $2,100"
2. **Countdown Reminder (Day 10, 12, 14):** Trial ending, here's what you'll lose
3. **Annual Discount Pop-up:** Save 17% on annual (shown on Day 12)
4. **Downgrade Warning:** Show exactly what features they'll lose
5. **Win-Back (Day 21):** "Still want to save 10 hours/week? Here's 20% off"
